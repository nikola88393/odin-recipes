Lemon tart recipe

Homemade Lemon Tart

Lemon Tart, anyone? With a buttery crust and a zingy lemon filling, this tart is ready for the dessert table! Serve it for Easter, Mother's Day, or any time you need a special treat.

Ingredients

    For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups (200g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (50g) powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick, or 1/4 pound, 112g) unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

    • For the lemon filling:

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup creme fraiche (can sub sour cream)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon zest

Steps to Cook

  1. Make the tart dough
  2. Chill the tart crust
  3. Preheat the oven
  4. Roll out the tart crust
  5. Prebake the tart crust
  6. Make the lemon curd filling
  7. Fill and bake the tart
  8. Chill before serving
